Left or Right? The Link Between Political Orientation and Partner Preferences in an International Sample of Single Women

Country Distribution of Participants
  1. First we wrangled the data.
  2. We decided which data to keep. See the codebook for further details
  3. Then we looked at some descriptives.
  4. The main analyses: We analyzed effects of right-wing political orientation on partner preferences.
  5. Then we ran some robustness analyses additionally including age as a covariate in each model.
  6. Additionally, we conducted exploratory analyses as we we were interested in the heterogeneity in relationships between countries.
  7. Finally, we provide supplementary analyses with a focus on preferred relationship options.
