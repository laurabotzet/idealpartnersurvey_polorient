H1a(2) Quadratic Effect

Here, we are examining the quadratic effect of right-wing political orientation on preferred political similarity in a partner using the Two Lines Approach (Simonsohn, 2018). We are using the Robin Hood Algorithm in order to set the breaking point. Then, we are calculating two multilevel regressions on either side of the breaking point. Outcome: Preference ratings for partnerâ€™s similar political beliefs and values. Predictors: Political Orientation & Age. Random intercept and random slope for country.

Algorithm: Find breaking point

See 11_twolines_analyses_multilevel
